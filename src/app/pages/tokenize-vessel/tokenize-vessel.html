How Vessel Tokenization Works

directions_boat

1. Digital Vessel Registration

Your physical vessel is first verified and registered on our secure platform. Key data such as ownership, inspection reports, and valuation are converted into a certified digital profile, ensuring trust and authenticity.

2. Token Creation on Blockchain

The vessel’s value is divided into digital tokens — each representing a fractional share of ownership. These tokens are securely stored on the blockchain, guaranteeing transparency, immutability, and real-time traceability.

3. Global Investment Access

Once tokenized, investors worldwide can buy, sell, or trade shares of your vessel. You gain liquidity, shared ownership benefits, and access to a broader investor base — all while maintaining transparent operations and automated revenue distribution.
